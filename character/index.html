Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is a blue hedgehog who can run past supersonic speeds. He is the protagonist of the Sonic the Hedgehog series. Usually, he will be tasked with foiling the various plots of Doctor Eggman, encountering hordes of enemies and dangerous areas as he progresses through the games.
